Sandwich au Beurre de Cacahuète et à la Confiture

It's PB&J... What did youi expect? Something fancy?

Ingredients:

  • Sliced bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly or jam of any fruit

Instructions:

  1. Prepare 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the first slice.
  3. Spread jam or jelly on the other slice.
  4. Lay the peanut butter side of the first slice onto the jelly side of the second slice.
  5. All done!
  6. Should you only have one slice of bread, you can Spread the peanut butter and jelly onto the two halves of the slice. Then, simply fold the peanut butter into the jelly.
