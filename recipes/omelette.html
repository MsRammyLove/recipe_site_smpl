Omelette

"A scintilating deli meat omellete to dance upon your tongue."

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Sliced ham
  • Chives(optional)

Directions:

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl. Stir until yolk and white are a consistent color.
  2. Spray non stick spray into your pan.
  3. Chop your ham into smaller bits. Add to your mixed yolk.
  4. Pour eggs and ham into the pan. Heat on medium to high heat.
  5. Add chives to the omelette when close to complete.
  6. All done! Go ahead and dish up. Add salt and pepper to taste.
