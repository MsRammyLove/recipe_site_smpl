Pan Pizza

A piquant personal pan pizza for alliterative folks.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh block of mozzarella (not shredded)
  • Pepperoni
  • 2 jalapenos
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce

Instructions:

CAUTION: Make sure your pan is oven safe!

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375.
  2. Start by cooking the pepperoni in a pan. This will make them crispy.
  3. Wash and slice your jalapenos.
  4. Add an even coating of olive oil to your main pan.
  5. Spread out your dough evenly in the pan.
  6. Add your two cups of marinara to the pizza.
  7. Tear some chunks of the mozzarella and add them to the pizza.
  8. Add your jalapenos and pepperoni.
  9. Add more mozzarella.
  10. Leave in the oven at 375 for 25-30 minutes.
  11. All done!
